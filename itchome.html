Overview

The website aims to provide knowledge about different starting topics in computer science, specifically, under the Introduction to Computing unit. This includes topics such as the different fields in computing, different information systems, on what information and what data is, different number systems, the different computer hardware and software, all about the different types of motherboards, and different types of computers. It does not go in-depth but provides the general overview of what these topics are and what these topics will cover. These topics are fundamental into stepping in to the field of computer studies, hence, having knowledge on them is a way to create a stepping stone when majoring in it.
